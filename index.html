What is Sabię?

Sabię is a TravelTech platform designed to elevate the entire travel journey, combining social connection, sustainable choices, and AI-driven planning. From finding eco-conscious accommodations to coordinating group trips and discovering local culture, Sabię empowers users to travel responsibly, seamlessly, and meaningfully.

How does Sabię differentiate itself in the TravelTech market?

it goes beyond simple bookings, offering AI-driven collaborative planning, financial tools, and mood-based recommendations to create an engaging, personalized, and sustainable travel experience.

How does Sabię assist with group trip planning?

Sabię offers collaborative tools where users can plan trips with friends, share suggestions, and manage budgets all within a group chat interface.

What makes Sabię’s AI different from other travel platforms?

Sabię’s AI provides tailored recommendations, real-time updates, and financial insights to help users make smarter travel decisions. It also promotes responsible tourism by highlighting sustainable choices and showing estimated carbon impact for each booking, helping users make eco-conscious decisions. Fun, mood-based filters further personalize the experience, making travel planning engaging and meaningful.

How does Sabię ensure the security and authenticity of listings?

Our multi-layered verification process includes on-site inspections, documentation reviews, and blockchain technology to certify the authenticity of every listing and protect against fraud.

How does Sabię ensure protection of user data?

Sabię leverages cutting-edge encryption and blockchain technologies to secure user transactions and safeguard sensitive information. Our platform's built-in transparency features include a secure, tamper-proof ledger, offering top-level data protection against breaches. This approach ensures a trusted and secure environment for all your dealings, providing peace of mind with every transaction.

Can Sabię help manage travel expenses?

Yes, Sabie includes real-time budget tracking, expense splitting, and cost-saving suggestions for group travel, all integrated into a user-friendly travel wallet.
