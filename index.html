Questions?

What is Sabię?

Sabię is an innovative PropTech platform designed to streamline real estate transactions and property management with state-of-the-art technology.

What will Sabię's pricing structure be like?

Stay tuned for Sabię's upcoming pricing plans, which are being carefully crafted to provide exceptional value for property owners and renters, ensuring affordability and transparency.

How does Sabię differentiate itself in the PropTech market?

Sabię stands out with its commitment to harnessing the latest technologies for enhanced user experience, including virtual property tours and AI-driven property matching, all while prioritizing user-centric design and community engagement.

What is the expected launch date for Sabię, and how can I get early access?

We're excited to bring Sabię to market soon. Sign up now for early access to be one of the first to explore our platform's capabilities and to participate in our beta testing program.

What types of properties and transactions can users expect to handle through Sabię?

Sabię will offer a comprehensive listing of residential and commercial properties for buying, renting, and investment, along with innovative features like asset tokenization for fractional ownership.

How does Sabię ensure the security and authenticity of listings?

Our multi-layered verification process includes on-site inspections, documentation reviews, and blockchain technology to certify the authenticity of every listing and protect against fraud.

Can I contribute to Sabię's platform development?

Absolutely! We encourage community feedback and involvement through our early access programs, ensuring that Sabię is built with direct input from its future users.

Will Sabię support international real estate investors, and how?

Yes, Sabię is designed with global investors in mind, featuring multi-currency support, cross-border transaction facilitation, and comprehensive market analytics to inform investment decisions.
