About JobEx

Welcome to JobEx, a professional network platform that is here to assist Ex Convit secure jobs. With more than 30 million invested in this company, we would treat everyone with the respect that they deserve. We have our Head Office in London and are about to lunch other operations in more than 200 countries worldwide.

Vision

Create economic opportunity for every member of the global workforce.

Mission

The mission of JobEx is simple: connect the world’s professionals to make them more productive and successful.

Who are we?

JobEx began in co-founder Nicholas Thurman's living room in early 2022 and was officially launched on November 11, 2022. JobEx leads a diversified business with revenues from membership subscriptions, advertising sales and recruitment solutions under the leadership of Giulia Pedrazzini. In November 2022, JobEx aquired three more directors, An Nyuyen, Federico Patruno and Julia Pankiewicz.
